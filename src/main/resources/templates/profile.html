About Me

Hello! I'm Alex, a passionate travel and lifestyle blogger based in San Francisco. After spending 5 years in corporate marketing, I decided to pursue my dream of exploring the world and sharing my experiences through storytelling and photography.

My blog focuses on authentic travel experiences, sustainable living, and finding beauty in everyday moments. Whether I'm hiking through remote mountains or discovering hidden gems in bustling cities, I aim to provide honest perspectives and practical tips for fellow adventurers.

When I'm not traveling, you can find me experimenting with new recipes, practicing yoga, or curled up with a good book and my cat, Luna.
